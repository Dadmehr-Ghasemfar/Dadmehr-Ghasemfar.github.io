One Pound Battle Robot

Tyler Bletsch Robotics Team

  Every semester, Dr. Tyler Bletsch - a professor of engineering at Duke University - hosts a 1lb robotics battle. Me and my friends decided to throw together a robot in a couple days and compete. The entire build consists of a 3D printed body, a metallic spinning bludgeon weapon, motors, ESC's, batteries, radio transciever, and homemade wheels (3D printed hub and acrylic tires)

Although we came last in the tournament, we had a lot of fun. We also had an opportunity to see the north american division champion, who ended up obliterating our build. This coming semester, we will improve upon the design and compete again.
